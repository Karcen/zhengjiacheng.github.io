About

Hi, thanks for stopping by! I am Jiacheng (Karcen) Zheng, an undergraduate (Class of 2022) in Bioengineering at the Marine College, Shandong University (Weihai), and an incoming master's student at the Ma Yinchu School of Economics, Tianjin University. I previously studied Chemical Engineering at Dalian University of Technology (2019–2021) and took part in a dual-degree program in International Politics and International Economics at the School of Northeast Asian Studies, Shandong University (2022–2023).

I worked as a research assistant at the Center for Data Analysis on Area and Country Studies, Institute of International Studies, Shandong University, supervised by Prof. Yuan Li and Dr. Jamal Khan (2023–2025). In summer 2024 I joined the Climate Change Economics & Low-Carbon Transition team at University College London, supervised by Prof. Zhifu Mi and Dr. Haoran Zhang, building the IPCC reference database.

In 2025 I was a research intern at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology for the Hong Kong Generative AI R&D Center (HKGAI), working on AI alignment for a multi-turn multimodal LLM with Jiaming Ji and Boyuan Chen from Peking University.
